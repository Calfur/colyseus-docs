# Authentication

Authentication is the process of verifying the identity of a user.

Colyseus provides built-in tools to help you authenticate your clients into your application. You may bring your own authentication if you prefer.

You may bring your own token validation logic, or use [<PackageIcon/> Auth Module](/auth/module/) to manage the authentication flow.
